WHO ARE WE?

Gauss Global Investment LLC is a Dubai-based company that is tackling a very specific problem: plastic.

Together, those responsible have more than 50 years of experience in building and creating networks, which made them develop a business, with an active hand in society, promoting recycling and fighting pollution, through the transformation of plastic into alternative fuel. This is a differentiating and innovative factor in building a network business.

Our goal is to create something that prompts enthusiasm and the will to continue to grow further, making a difference to the communities where we are inserted. This is how Gauss Global LLC was created and this is how it will continue to grow.

MISSION

We are committed to the environment, understanding its global problems but contributing to a real solution to save the planet. We know that the sacrifice and the price that you are not willing to pay will end up being paid by your children or grandchildren; with that in mind, the future starts today, the future is green, the future from now on , is GAUSS.

VISION

Allow our members to benefit from the growth of their businesses in an unprecedented way, providing a positive, structured and professional environment for their personal development and growth and the respective communities.

Dionísio

da Silva Simões

CEO

Graduated from accounting, Dionísio has always been passionate about numbers. After years of working in the area, he has decided to turn his life around and follow his dream of having a more autonomous career, where his happiness was not dependent of a 9-6 job. At 45 years of age, he has changed his path, and decided to invest not only on working towards his personal growth but also on what really matters: spending time with his loved ones.

Over the years, Dionisio has done numerous courses that allowed him to develop his strengths and work on his weaknesses. Those include neuro-linguistic programming, neuro-strategy, and coaching. With that in mind, Dionísio is not scared of taking bold and brave decisions. He has spent a couple of years in Brazil inspiring thousands of people on how to stop complaining and start working towards a more fulfilled life. His experience speaks for itself, after reaching higher positions in several network marketing companies, he put into theory what he had been able to do in practice: develop leaders.

MARTIN ROJAS

GENERAL DIRECTOR

From a very young age, Martin has led a very disciplined life. He went through military training for 3 years, which allowed him to face life and its adversities with a different perspective. When he started his career in economics he began to do Marketing Multilevel and his life really started to change. He then decided to move to another country in search of a better life for him and his loved ones. The opportunities within this world were endless, and Martin was able to navigate the industry better than anyone else.

While creating a legacy and providing his loved ones with a better future, he was able to travel the world. In the process, he was exposed to over 50 countries, allowing him to see and approach the world with a very open and different mindset. Thanks to his life and work experiences, Martin has become an expert in networking and his knowledge in this area is unquestionable. His life motto is: who lives to serve, serves to live!
