WHO ARE WE?

Gauss Global Investment LLC is a Dubai-based company that is tackling a very specific problem: plastic.

Our directors have more than 50 years of experience in business, which made them develop a project with an active hand in society, promoting recycling and fighting pollution, through the transformation of plastic into an alternative to fuel.

Our goal is to create a business that prompts enthusiasm and that can make a difference to the communities where we are inserted. This is how Gauss Global LLC was created and this is how it will continue to grow.

MISSION

We are committed to the environment, understanding its global problems but contributing to a real solution to save the planet. We know that the sacrifice and the price that you are not willing to pay will end up being paid by your children or grandchildren; with that in mind, the future starts today, the future is green, the future from now on , is GAUSS.

VISION

Allow our members to benefit from the growth of their businesses in an unprecedented way, providing a positive, structured and professional environment for their personal development and growth and the respective communities.
