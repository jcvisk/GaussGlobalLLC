The Problem

We have all seen images like the ones above, and we were always told that we have to change our path and find different ways of acting towards the environment. At Gauss Global LLC we are already doing something that could change how we recycle and look at plastic.

The Solution

At Gauss Global LLC, we like to find solutions and never problems, so we decided to focus our business on recycling materials that are part of our daily lives and take hundreds of years to degrade. And how do we do that? We own a patent for a plant that transforms various forms of plastic into fuel, such as diesel and gasoline, that are so essential for our daily life.

Power Plants

Our recycling plant makes it possible to transform industrial and agricultural plastic, tires, and used oils into ready-to-use fuels, without the need for refining. In a safe and clean way, we use all excesses to power our system, eliminating harmful gases and transforming them into energy, thus also reducing production costs.
