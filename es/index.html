POWER PLANT


Our power plant is a facility that transforms plastic into fuel and that consequently has the potential to create jobs and generate benefits for the economy while being a way to eliminate plastic and save the environment. Chemical recycling is also much simpler, as when the plastic is heated to high temperatures, it is not necessary to separate the waste beforehand.
