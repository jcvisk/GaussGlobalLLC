Inauguration
of our office

March 31, 2022

When Gauss Global LLC was first created we have always wanted to cause a strong impact within the communities that we are working with. We are a family owned company, from Portugal and Peru, and we always like to dream big. That is what brought us to Dubai. What better city to be based in than the business capital of the world? A city that was made from desert sand and it is now one of the most powerful in the world. A city where there are opportunities and where people from every corner of the world choose to live.

For us Dubai means new beginnings, new opportunities, but mainly Dubai means a chance of doing something better for our planet. A chance of changing our lives, and yours too! And this is exactly why we have chosen to officially open our office here.

Our inauguration took place on the 21st of October of 2021. This was a day full of emotions, and it marks the official beginning of Gauss Global LLC in the UAE.
